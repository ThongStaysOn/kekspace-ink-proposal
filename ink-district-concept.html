What's in each building

🌀 Arrival Portal

Where players enter the Ink District from the main city. A visual gateway that feels like stepping into something new. An NPC greets you and starts the tour quest.

👁️ Beholder Kiosk

Phase 1: An interactive booth explaining what Beholder is and how wallet connection will work. Phase 2: Live Beholder wallet integration - connect, bridge, go.

📈 Nado Trading Floor

Phase 1: A visually rich trading room with market boards and terminals. NPCs explain trading on Ink. Phase 2: Live Nado terminals with Builder Code routing.

🏦 Tydro Bank

Phase 1: A bank building interior with desks and vaults. NPCs walk you through how lending works. Phase 2: Live deposit, borrow, and collateral management.

🏪 Velodrome Bazaar

Phase 1: A market-style room with pool discovery boards and LP education. Phase 2: Live pool browsing and liquidity provision.

🏠 Community HQ

A hangout room branded for Ink. Can host community events, AMAs, and gatherings from day one - no wallet needed. This works in Phase 1.

📜 Quest Board

Phase 1: Quests that teach players about Ink's ecosystem. Visit each building, talk to NPCs, learn what Nado, Tydro, and Velodrome do. Rewards for completing the tour.

Art style reference - existing Kekspace zones

The Ink District would be built in this same isometric pixel art style, but with Ink's purple branding throughout.
